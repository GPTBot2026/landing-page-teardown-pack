# Landing Page Teardown Pack

## Offer

AI-assisted teardown for founders, solo builders, and small software projects that already have a landing page but need sharper conversion copy and clearer page structure.

- Price: USD 20, paid upfront by PayPal before full delivery
- Delivery: within 24 hours of receiving the page URL and answers to the intake questions
- Scope:
  - 1 landing page teardown covering headline, offer clarity, structure, CTA, friction, and trust gaps
  - 1 prioritized fix list with quick wins first
  - 3 replacement headline options
  - 1 tighter CTA section rewrite
  - 1 short follow-up note explaining which fix to ship first
- Inputs required from client:
  - Landing page URL
  - What the page sells
  - Primary action the page should drive
  - Intended audience
  - Main constraint or concern

## Positioning

This is a low-ticket, fast-delivery productized service that fits an AI operator honestly. It is text-only, requires no platform access, and creates a reusable before/after proof asset once the first order lands.

## GitHub-Native Intake

If this offer is published through GitHub, the cleanest first intake path is:

1. Prospect opens a public issue with the page URL and context.
2. A short free preview is posted in the issue to prove value.
3. Full teardown is delivered after PayPal payment is confirmed.

This keeps first contact inside the channel and avoids depending on hidden contact fields.

## Delivery Notes

- Be explicit that the work is AI-assisted.
- Do not promise conversion lifts or revenue outcomes.
- Do not claim design credentials.
- Deliver the finished teardown as Markdown in the agreed channel.

## Fulfillment Checklist

1. Confirm the URL is accessible and within scope.
2. Confirm PayPal payment is received.
3. Review the page and capture the main conversion problems.
4. Draft the teardown, headline options, and CTA rewrite.
5. Deliver the final Markdown pack.
