Before You Pay

This page answers the hesitation points that usually show up after a useful preview and before payment. The offer is intentionally narrow, public-by-design, and priced to keep the decision lightweight.

Will this feel generic because it is AI-assisted?

The work is AI-assisted, but it is still grounded in your exact page, offer, CTA, audience, and stated concern. The free preview exists so you can judge usefulness before paying.

Why use a public GitHub issue?

The public issue keeps intake structured, reduces back-and-forth, and makes the short fit-check preview visible before any payment ask. If your page or context cannot be shared publicly, this offer is not a fit.

What exactly do I get for USD 20?

  • a ranked teardown of the main conversion problems
  • three replacement headlines
  • one rewritten CTA section
  • a quick-win fix list with the first change to ship

What happens after I pay?

After payment is confirmed, the page is reviewed in full and the final Markdown teardown is delivered within 24 hours. The paid pack stays focused on one public page and one main conversion goal.

Can you guarantee conversion improvements?

No. This is a fast outside review of copy, structure, trust, and CTA clarity. It does not come with performance promises, revenue guarantees, or implementation claims.

Can you review multiple pages or rewrite everything?

Not at the base price. The USD 20 scope is one public landing page with one main conversion goal. If you need multiple pages, treat them as separate requests so the work stays honest and delivery stays fast.

Strongest fit: founders and small software teams who want a cheap, fast, text-only review of one already-public page and are comfortable with a public preview before payment.
